## Description: 
Seasonal field survey, based on four sampling campaigns (October 2021, March 2022, June 2022 and August 2022) of destructively harvesting macrophyte material in the Tv√§rminne archipelago, Finland.    
Data were collected through the use of high precision scales, a flatbed scanner, WinRHIZO and imageJ (Rueden et al. 2017)
